# Wealthy - Financial Planner

A comprehensive financial planning system built with Google Spreadsheets to track income, expenses, savings, investments, and loans.

## Components

### Budget Tracker
- Monthly income and expenses
- Category-based spending analysis
- Savings goals and progress

### Investment Portfolio
- Asset allocation and diversification
- Performance tracking
- Risk assessment

### Loan Management
- Active loans (mortgages, personal loans, student loans, etc.)
- Payment schedules and amortization
- Interest calculations
- Payoff strategies and early payment scenarios

### Net Worth Calculator
- Assets vs. liabilities overview
- Historical tracking
- Financial health metrics

## Getting Started

1. Create a Google Spreadsheet with separate sheets for each component
2. Define your financial goals
3. Track all income sources
4. Categorize and monitor expenses
5. List all loans with terms and interest rates
6. Document investments and assets
7. Use formulas to calculate net worth automatically
8. Review and adjust monthly

## Spreadsheet Structure

- **Dashboard** - Overview with key metrics and charts
- **Income** - Monthly income tracking
- **Expenses** - Categorized expense log
- **Budget** - Planned vs. actual spending
- **Loans** - Loan details, payment schedules, and amortization tables
- **Investments** - Portfolio holdings and performance
- **Net Worth** - Assets and liabilities summary
